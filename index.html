About Xenopsychology

Xenopsychology is the study of the psychological nature of non-human or hypothetical minds — from animals and artificial intelligences to theoretical extraterrestrial intelligences.

This website serves as a hub for research, articles, and discussions surrounding the science and philosophy of minds beyond the human experience.

Key Topics

Animal Cognition — How do non-human animals perceive, reason, and feel?

Artificial Intelligence — Can machines develop genuine psychological states?

Astrobiology & SETI — What might the psychology of extraterrestrial life look like?

Philosophy of Mind — Consciousness, qualia, and the hard problem applied beyond humanity.
